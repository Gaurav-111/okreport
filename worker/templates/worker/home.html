About OkReport

OkReport is a smart and user-friendly web application designed to simplify daily worker management for local builders, contractors, and site supervisors. With OkReport, registered users can easily add and categorize their workforce into helpers and proficient workers, assign daily wages, and manage attendance with just a few clicks. system automatically tracks daily attendance and calculates monthly salaries based on actual working days, eliminating the hassle of manual record-keeping or spreadsheets. Whether you're managing a small team or multiple laborers across different sites, OkReport provides a centralized, efficient solution to keep your workforce organized and your reporting accurate. Built using Django, the platform is secure, fast, and tailored for practical, real-world construction site needs.
